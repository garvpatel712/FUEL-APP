FAQs

Discover how our fuel delivery service works and get answers to your common questions.

How does delivery work?
Our delivery service allows you to order fuel online. Once you place your order, our team will process it and dispatch a delivery driver to your location. You can track the delivery in real-time for convenience.
What payment methods are available?
We offer various secure payment options including GPay, PayPal, Net Banking, UPI, and Cash on Delivery. This flexibility ensures that you can choose the method that works best for you. Your payment information is always kept safe and secure.
Can I track my order?
Yes, you can track your order in real-time through our platform. An interactive map will show you the delivery progress and estimated arrival time. This feature ensures transparency and keeps you informed.
What if I have issues?
If you encounter any issues, our customer support team is here to help. You can reach out via the contact button for assistance. We are committed to resolving your concerns promptly.
How to change orders?
To change your order, please contact our support team as soon as possible. We will do our best to accommodate your request before the order is processed. Changes may not be possible once the delivery is en route.
